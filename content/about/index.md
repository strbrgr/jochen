# About

Hello, I'm Jochen! Originally from Germany, I have enjoyed life on 3 different continents. In 2017, I left Germany for China and came to the United States in January of 2020 on one of the last flights before the world stood still.

Before transitioning into software engineering, I was a photographer and artist working and exhibiting in Germany, France, and China. Once the pandemic hit, I realized that it would be difficult to find freelance gigs while most of the country was locked down. So, I transitioned into software engineering as I have always been coding on the side.

I'm self-taught; I picked up everything on my own. If given the choice, I would write Rust, but in the end, a language is just a tool and all I care about is impact.
